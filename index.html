Hello and Welcome to Poisoned Minds!


This is the main page of the website and will house all of the general rules associated with being in this clan.

  • Try to maintain a 1:1 donation ratio, this means that your troops donated should be approximatly equal to your troops recieved.

  • The base troop for donations is the archer. This means if you don't ask for a specific troop, you will most likely get archers.

  • During war, donations will become very specific, and must not be filled with any other troop. For more on this and other War specific rules, visit the war page here.

  • Outside of wars, if troop donations last for more than ten minutes, archers may be donated to that person.

  • While there are no specific requirements for the clan outside of one thousand trophies, we will only accept people who will make a valuable addition to our clan.

  • In order to become a clan elder or co-leader, you must show loyalty and dedication to the clan, in addition to offering help and advice to clanmates.

  • And finally don't be rude to others in the clan, its easier for everyone when manners are used.


Our main 2 general leaders are Monkeysocks and Convel, monkey is located in the UK and convel is located in the Pacific US, this allows for a leader to be awake for almost all of the day

While moneky and con take care of the general clan, Paraisle1, Jack and Sleepless are in charge of the wars, often called 'War Generals'

The War Generals will come up with a plan of attack for the war and assign targets for second attacks depending on the current progress of the war.
